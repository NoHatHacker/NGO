Humming Bird

Humming Bird Is A Non Governmental Organization Created By The Youths (Mostly Students) From Barpeta Road (Assam).


This NGO Primarily Focuses On Helping Poor People & Children As It's Core Policy Says - Relentless Compassion, Unstoppable Aid.


Moreover, This NGO Plays A Major Role In Providing Emergency Funds To The Suffering One's.


The Workers Of This NGO Believes In Transforming This NGO Into A Mass Movement Which Conveys The Message Of World Peace 🕊️ 💕.
